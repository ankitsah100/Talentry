About Talentry

Nepal's first AI-powered healthcare job portal — connecting healthcare professionals with opportunities across Nepal.

Our Mission

Talentry was built to solve a real problem: Nepal's healthcare sector desperately needs skilled professionals, but there was no dedicated platform connecting them. We bridge that gap with modern technology and AI.

We serve biomedical engineers, nurses, lab technicians, doctors, radiographers, and all healthcare workers — helping them find meaningful work across hospitals, clinics, and NGOs in Nepal.

What We Offer

🔍 Smart job search with AI matching
📄 AI-powered CV parsing and resume builder
🤝 Interview prep with AI coaching
💰 Salary insights for Nepal's healthcare sector
🏥 Direct connection with top hospitals and clinics
