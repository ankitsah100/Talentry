How to earn more

📈
Get certified
Additional certifications in your specialty can increase salary by 15–30%. In Nepal, international certifications (WHO, USAID training) are especially valued by INGOs and private hospitals.
🏥
Target INGOs and NGOs
International organizations like WHO, UNICEF, Save the Children typically pay 2–3x more than local private hospitals. They also offer USD-based salaries, benefits, and career growth.
💬
Negotiate with data
Come to salary discussions with data from this page. Knowing the market range gives you confidence to ask for 20–30% more than the initial offer — most employers expect negotiation.
📍
Location matters
Kathmandu and Lalitpur pay significantly more than other cities. Remote areas sometimes offer hardship allowances that increase effective salary by 25–40% on top of base pay.
