Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026 · We take your privacy seriously

Talentry is built for Nepal's healthcare community. We only collect what we need to connect you with the right jobs and employers.

What data we collect

Data typeWhy we collect it
Name, email, phoneAccount creation and job applications
Location (city)Match you with nearby jobs
Job title, skills, experienceAI job matching and recommendations
Resume/CV uploadsAI parsing to auto-fill applications
Interview session dataImprove your practice scores over time
Usage dataImprove platform features

How we use your data

  • Match you with relevant healthcare jobs in Nepal
  • Power our AI tools: job matching, interview coach, salary insights
  • Allow employers to view your profile when you apply
  • Send job alerts (only if you opt in)
  • Improve our AI models and platform features

Who we share data with

We only share your data with:

  • Employers you apply to — they see your application details
  • Supabase — our secure database provider
  • Anthropic (Claude AI) — powers our AI features (no personal data stored by Anthropic)
  • ElevenLabs — powers AI voice in video interviews

We never sell your personal data to third parties.

Your rights

  • Access your data anytime from your profile
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Opt out of marketing emails
  • Request a copy of your data

Data security

Your data is stored securely on Supabase infrastructure with end-to-end encryption. Passwords are hashed using bcrypt — we never store plain text passwords. We follow industry security standards.

Cookies

We use minimal cookies for authentication only. We do not use advertising cookies or third-party tracking.

Contact

For privacy concerns: privacy@talentry.com.np
